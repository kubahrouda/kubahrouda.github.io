Birds in power-line corridors: effects of vegetation mowing on avian diversity and abundance

Power line corridors in forested areas positively impact bird communities, increasing both species richness and abundance compared to surrounding forests - partially by supporting open-habitat specialists.

Poster: Seasonal changes in habitat preferences of migratory birds

Using species distribution modeling (SDM) to analyze habitat preferences of migratory birds, ultimately working towards a comprehensive algorithm for predicting species occurrence and informing conservation efforts.

Presented at EOU Fledglings 2024 and Zoologické dny 2024; awarded the CSO prize for best student ornithological poster
